Cognoscentia

Cognoscentia is the flagship quizzing event of Sarasva which is organized annually as a part of the cultural fest, Effervescence. A mind boggling and insanely competitive inter-college quiz, the event aims to bring out the genius in the participants as they attempt to answer mind numbing questions.

The participants engage in a battle of wits amidst intense competition. Boasting of a meticulously prepared question bank, this taxing event is known to test the mettle of even the most experienced quizzers as they not only face perplexing questions, but also need nerves of steel as they take part in intense tie breakers with equally talented opposition.

Apart from the amazing experience of slugging it out with the best minds of the country, the lucrative prizes provide another incentive for the teams to perform to their limits. This event is sure to be an enlightening experience for the participants and audience alike.
