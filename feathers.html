Feathers

“The true alchemists do not turn lead into gold, they change the world into words.”

This quote in itself is the core essence of Feathers, an annual writing event organized by Sarasva. This event encourages young minds to let loose their thoughts on paper. Ever since its inception, Feathers has received widespread participation and exceptional entries every year.

The competition is divided into 2 rounds. For round one, participants have to write short 20-50 word tiny tales on one of the given set of pictures and words and submit them to Sarasva. The best stories are chosen and converted into posters to be published on the official Facebook page. The second round is an offline round which is open for all. The participants who earned bonus points in the first round get their scores added to this round. The person with the best cumulative score is declared the winner.
