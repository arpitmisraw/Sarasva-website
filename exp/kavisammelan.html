Kavi Sammelan

Previous Next

Kavi Sammelan is a poem recitation program, performed by amazing poets and shayars of the college on the Foundation Day.

Foundation Day is the perfect starter program for the freshmen to showcase their extra curricular skills. It comprises of singing, dancing, poem recitation, drama etc, performed by the freshmen themselves.

Kavi Sammelan is one of the most loved events of the day, with audience cheering in unison after the delivery of every single line! The poets take turn to present their unique and often student-life-related poems that are hilarious and heartfelt at the same time. The Hindi poems and Shayaris are on a plethora of topics and genres, ranging from prevalant issues in society to comic poems to love and heartbreak. The poets leave you baffled with their enriching performances.
